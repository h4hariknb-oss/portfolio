Go-Kart Design Challenge 2024

The Go-Kart Design Challenge (GKDC) 2024, organized by ISNEE Motorsports, is the 11th edition of the competition. It is held at the Kari Motor Speedway in Coimbatore. The GKDC is a design challenge where teams design, build, and race go-karts, showcasing their engineering skills and passion for motorsports. Worked on chassis development, drivetrain integration, and steering system optimization. Participated in GDC 2024 with a complete working prototype.

SAE H-BAJA 2025

BAJA SAEINDIA 2025, the 18th edition, is a student design competition where engineering students design and build all-terrain vehicles (ATVs). The competition emphasizes four key areas: technology, diversity, sustainability, and connectivity. This year's event will feature both physical and virtual rounds, with a focus on sustainable practices like HCNG fuel (Hydrogen-CNG) and autonomous vehicle technologies.
